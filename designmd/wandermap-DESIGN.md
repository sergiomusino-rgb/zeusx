# WanderMap Design System

## Overview

WanderMap is an adventurous, photo-rich design system for travel planning and destination discovery platforms. It celebrates exploration with a vibrant teal-coral palette, generous imagery, and rounded, friendly components.

## Colors

- **Primary** (#0F766E): Primary actions, map pins
- **Secondary** (#F97316): CTAs, featured trips
- **Tertiary** (#0EA5E9): Links, sky accents
- **Background** (#FFFFFF): Page background
- **Surface** (#FFFFFF): Cards, modals
- **Success** (#059669)
- **Warning** (#D97706)
- **Error** (#EF4444)
- **Info** (#0EA5E9)

## Do's and Don'ts
- Do lead with large, high-quality destination photography
- Don't use auto-scrolling image carousels
- Do include map integration with teal pins for consistency
